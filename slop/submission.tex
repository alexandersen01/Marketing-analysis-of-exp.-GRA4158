\textbf{Problem Statement:}

This metastudy quantifies the prevalence of low-quality academic output -- ``slop'' -- across disciplines. Using data science techniques, we develop an eigen-slop parameter to identify and measure patterns of research degradation, comparing cross-field differences and investigating their underlying drivers.
